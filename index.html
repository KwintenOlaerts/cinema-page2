What is 248?

248 is nothing more than a state of mind, shared by a group of people. The original founder was Kwinten Olaerts, also known as Lerr.
After letting the vision grow and manifest he shared his idea with his good friend and college Lander Willems.
Completely embracing this mind-state, together they formed a music group with an emphasis on their beliefs.
Hard work, always having fun, not letting doubt create mental blocks and knowing that succes is something that adds up, just like compound intrest.

It is a metafor, which is substracted from the well known '1,2,3'.
The truth is, what holds value doesn't come easy and what is easy rarely holds value.
We love the think we can fix things in a hurry, but we should embrace the the progress.
And realize that the further we go in something, the harder it is to reach the next step.
But luckely, as it gets harder every step, the rewards get bigger too.
First you go from 2 to 4, then you have to fill in a bigger gap, but get a bigger reward.
It is for everybody who works hard, too make em keep the faith and embrace this valuable mindset.
